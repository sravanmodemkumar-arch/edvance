# EduForge — Group 4: College Level Roles

> Covers intermediate / junior colleges (Class 11–12) and degree colleges.
> Scale: 800 colleges · 150 to 2,000 students per college.
> Boards: BIEAP · TSBIE · CBSE · ICSE · State boards.
> Streams: MPC · BiPC · MEC · CEC · HEC + Integrated JEE/NEET.

---

## Two Types of Colleges — Platform Must Support Both

### Large College (Residential + Integrated Coaching)
> 800–2,000 students · Boys + Girls hostels · Integrated JEE/NEET
- Dedicated HOD per stream (MPC, BiPC, MEC, CEC)
- Separate JEE/NEET faculty parallel to board curriculum
- Boys hostel + Girls hostel (separate campuses often)
- AC and Non-AC hostel blocks
- Full labs: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer
- IQAC cell for quality assurance
- Placement coordinator for degree programs
- Research projects + science fairs
- NAAC/NBA accreditation compliance
- EAMCET/JEE rank-based seat counselling support

### Small College (Day Only)
> 150–500 students · Day scholars · Single or two streams
- Principal covers most admin
- One lecturer per subject
- No hostel or 30–50 hostelers max
- No integrated coaching
- Single stream (MPC or BiPC)
- Basic lab setup
- No dedicated placement/research cell

## College vs School — Key Differences

| Aspect | School (Group 3) | College (Group 4) |
|---|---|---|
| Student age | 6–16 years | 16–18 years |
| Teachers called | Teachers | Lecturers |
| Streams | Single curriculum | MPC / BiPC / MEC / CEC / HEC |
| Parallel coaching | Rare | Integrated JEE/NEET common |
| Board | CBSE / State SSC | BIEAP / TSBIE / CBSE |
| Compliance body | NCERT / SCERT | UGC / BIEAP / NAAC |
| Accreditation | Optional | NAAC mandatory for degree |
| Student self-access | Limited (minor) | Full self-access (16–18) |
| Career counselling | General guidance | EAMCET / JEE rank counselling |
| Research | Science fair only | Research projects, NPTEL |
| Alumni | School reunions | Engineering/Medical college referrals |
| Anti-ragging | Optional | UGC mandatory compliance |
| Parent dependence | High | Moderate (students more independent) |
