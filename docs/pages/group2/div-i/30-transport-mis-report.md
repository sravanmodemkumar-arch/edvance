## 1. Purpose

Monthly Management Information System (MIS) report for transport operations. Consolidates all key transport metrics into a single exportable report for the Group Chairman, Board, and CFO. The Transport Director reviews and approves the MIS before distribution.

The MIS report is typically prepared by the 5th of each month for the previous month. It covers: fleet size and status, route summary, student ridership, safety incidents, fee collection, fuel cost, driver compliance, and GPS coverage statistics.

## 4. Report Generator

### 4.1 Report Configuration

**Fields:** Month · Year · Include Branches (All / Select) · Include Sections (checkboxes)

**Sections available:**
- ✅ Fleet Summary (vehicle count, operational, under maintenance, compliance score)
- ✅ Route Summary (routes, students, on-time performance)
- ✅ GPS Coverage (uptime %)
- ✅ Safety Incidents (by severity, by branch, corrective actions)
- ✅ Driver Compliance (licence, BGV, training %)
- ✅ Transport Fee Collection (billed, collected, outstanding, defaulters)
- ✅ Fuel & Operational Cost (total, per student, efficiency)
- ✅ Key Issues & Actions (manually entered summary by Transport Director)
