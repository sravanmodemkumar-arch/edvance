## Overview

Division O covers the complete marketing, brand management, and admissions campaign function for an Institution Group. It encompasses brand identity and standards enforcement, multi-channel admissions campaign planning and execution (newspaper, digital, WhatsApp, outdoor), lead pipeline management from first enquiry to confirmed enrollment, telecalling and follow-up operations, cross-branch enrollment drives with seat-fill targets, topper relations and brand ambassador programmes, PR and media management, referral tracking, school fair and open-day coordination, and full campaign analytics with ROI attribution.

In the Indian education market — particularly for large chains like Narayana, Sri Chaitanya, or Tirumala — admissions are the lifeblood. A group that fills 95% seats by April wins the revenue battle for the entire academic year. A group that limps to 70% by July loses buses, hostels, and teacher salaries become unsustainable. The difference between these outcomes is marketing execution: how fast enquiries convert, how effectively toppers are showcased, and how each branch's local catchment is penetrated through the right mix of newspaper ads, WhatsApp blasts, and parent referrals.

### Critical Context: G0 vs G3 Split

Most original marketing roles (114–118) are **G0 — No Platform Access**. These roles operate in external tools: Google Ads, Meta Business Suite, Canva, WhatsApp Business API, PR agencies, newspaper ad agencies. The platform does NOT replace those external tools.

However, the platform serves three critical functions that external tools cannot:

1. **Lead-to-Admission Pipeline (G3):** Enquiry data, application records, seat matrices, fee payment confirmations — all live in EduForge. No external CRM can close this loop. Role 119 (Admissions Campaign Manager) and Role 130 (Admission Telecaller Executive) are the primary platform operators.
2. **Topper Relations (G3):** Board results, JEE/NEET ranks, student profiles with photos — all live in EduForge. Role 120 (Topper Relations Manager) builds the showcase and brand ambassador programme from this data.
3. **Campaign Analytics (G1):** Connecting ad spend to actual enrolled students requires platform data. Role 132 (Admission Data Analyst) produces the ROI reports that justify marketing budgets.

G4/G5 roles (CEO, Chairman) have full read access to all marketing analytics for strategic decisions.

| Profile | Branches | Students | Staffing Model |

| Small Group (SME Trust) | 5–10 | 2,000–8,000 | 2–3 people covering marketing + admissions; owner handles brand/PR; 1 telecaller; no dedicated topper or analytics roles |
