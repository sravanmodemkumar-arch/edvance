# D-01 — Notes Library

> **URL:** `/student/learn/notes`
> **File:** `d-01-notes-library.md`
> **Priority:** P1
> **Roles:** Student (S1–S6) · Content Partner (supplies notes) · Institution (curates for their students)

---

## Overview

Centralised repository of study notes covering all subjects across all supported exams — organised by exam → subject → topic → sub-topic. Notes are authored by EduForge's content partner network (Group 9) and curated by the EduForge content team. Available in English, Telugu, Hindi, and 8 other regional languages. Free students can read notes online; Premium students can download PDFs for offline access. Institution-specific notes (school textbook summaries, coaching handouts) are uploaded by the institution and visible only to their students.

## 3. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|--------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/notes?exam={exam}&subject={subject}` | Browse notes by exam/subject |
| 2 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/notes/{note_id}` | Note metadata (title, author, pages, ratings) |
| 3 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/notes/{note_id}/content?page={page}&lang={lang}` | Note page content |
| 4 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/notes/{note_id}/pdf` | Download PDF (Premium only) |
| 5 | `POST` | `/api/v1/student/notes/{note_id}/bookmark` | Bookmark a note |
| 6 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/notes/bookmarks` | List bookmarked notes |
| 7 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/notes/institution/{inst_id}` | Notes uploaded by the student's institution |
| 8 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/notes/recommended` | AI-recommended notes based on weak topics |

## 4. Business Rules

- Notes are organised in a three-tier hierarchy: exam → subject → topic — a student browsing "SSC CGL → General Knowledge → Indian Economy" sees 4–6 note sets covering the topic from different content partners; the system ranks note sets by: (a) rating (student reviews), (b) relevance to the student's weak areas, (c) recency (recently updated notes rank higher), and (d) language match (Telugu-language notes rank higher for AP/TS students who set Telugu as preferred language).

- PDF download is a Premium feature — Free students can read all notes online in the browser reader but cannot download; this is enforced server-side (the `/pdf` endpoint returns 403 for Free tier); the rationale: online reading generates engagement metrics (time spent, pages read) that feed into the AI study plan, while offline PDFs are "fire and forget" — Premium students who download still get recommendations based on their practice/test performance.

- Institution-specific notes are isolated — Sri Chaitanya's faculty-uploaded notes are visible ONLY to students linked to Sri Chaitanya; other students cannot see or search for them; this respects the institution's intellectual property and prevents coaching centres' proprietary notes from leaking to competitors; institution notes appear in a separate "Institution Notes" section below the EduForge shared library.

- Content partners earn revenue when students read their notes — the revenue model mirrors the question revenue model (Group 9): ₹0.01 per student per note per month; Dr. Venkat Rao's Mechanics notes read by 18,000 students generates ₹180/month from notes alone; this incentivises content partners to produce high-quality, well-maintained notes that attract readers.
