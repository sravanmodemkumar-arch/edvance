# D-02 — Video Library

> **URL:** `/student/learn/videos`
> **File:** `d-02-video-library.md`
> **Priority:** P1
> **Roles:** Student (S5–S6, Premium only) · Institution (curates playlists for their students)

---

## Overview

4,200+ hours of video content covering all subjects for all supported exams — concept lectures, problem-solving sessions, formula revision, and exam strategy videos. Videos are curated from EduForge's content partner network and top educators. Available in English, Telugu, and Hindi. The video library is a **Premium-only feature** — Free students see video thumbnails and previews (first 2 minutes) but need Premium for full access. Institution students whose coaching has gifted Premium can access the full library. Videos are linked contextually from notes, weak topics, and test result pages.

## 3. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|--------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/videos?exam={exam}&subject={subject}` | Browse videos |

| 3 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/videos/{video_id}` | Video details + chapters |
| 4 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/videos/{video_id}/stream` | HLS streaming URL (Premium check) |
| 5 | `POST` | `/api/v1/student/videos/{video_id}/progress` | Save watch progress (timestamp) |

| 8 | `POST` | `/api/v1/student/videos/{video_id}/rate` | Rate video (1–5 stars) |

## 4. Business Rules

- Video streaming uses adaptive bitrate (HLS) via CloudFront CDN with 22 edge locations across India — the player auto-detects connection speed and serves 360p (3G/slow 4G), 480p (4G), or 720p (Wi-Fi/fast 4G); 1080p is available but rarely used since 78% of students watch on mobile screens where 720p is indistinguishable; initial buffering must be under 2 seconds on 4G; the player pre-buffers 30 seconds ahead to handle intermittent connectivity.

- Video access is gated at the streaming URL level — the `/stream` endpoint validates the student's subscription status before returning the HLS manifest URL; Free students get a preview (first 2 minutes of any video + a "Subscribe for full access" overlay); the HLS URLs are signed with a 4-hour TTL to prevent URL sharing; DRM (Widevine on Android, FairPlay on iOS) is applied to prevent screen recording of Premium content.

- Watch progress is tracked per-student per-video — the player sends progress updates every 30 seconds; this data feeds into the AI study plan ("Ravi watched 48% of Organic Chemistry Masterclass — still needs to complete chapters on Friedel-Crafts and Electrophilic Substitution"); the "Continue Watching" section on the library home uses this data to show the resume point.

- Content partners earn ₹0.005 per student per minute of video watched — Prof. Amit Sharma's Organic Chemistry Masterclass (2h 15m = 135 min), watched by 18,000 students averaging 80 min watched each = 18,000 × 80 × ₹0.005 = ₹7,200/month from this single video; this incentivises high-quality, engaging content that students actually watch to completion.
