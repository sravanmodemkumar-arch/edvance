> **URL:** `/student/fees/scholarships`
> **File:** `e-04-scholarship-financial-aid.md`
> **Priority:** P2
> **Roles:** Student (S2–S6) · Institution (awards institution scholarships) · EduForge (awards platform scholarships)

---

## Overview

Discovery and application portal for scholarships and financial aid — both EduForge platform scholarships (merit-based Premium access, SC/ST/EWS subsidies) and institution-specific scholarships (fee waivers, merit awards). Also aggregates external government schemes (PMKVY, Skill India, state welfare schemes) that students can apply for. For many students from economically weaker backgrounds in Tier-2/3 towns, this page is the difference between continuing their exam preparation and dropping out.

## 4. Business Rules

- EduForge Merit Scholarship (80%+ in first 3 mocks → 6 months free Premium) has a deliberate 80% threshold that is achievable but not trivial — it requires genuine preparation and acts as a quality filter; 12% of new students earn the scholarship; renewal requires maintaining 75%+ in any 3 tests during the scholarship period — this is slightly lower than the initial threshold to avoid punishing students who have a bad test day; the scholarship converts 34% of merit scholars into paid Premium subscribers when the free period ends because they've experienced the value.

- SC/ST/EWS subsidy at ₹99/year (vs ₹2,499 retail) is funded from EduForge's corporate social responsibility (CSR) budget — the ₹99 covers payment gateway fees and serves as a nominal commitment to reduce sign-up fraud; income certificate verification is manual (EduForge operations team reviews uploaded certificates within 5 business days); false declarations detected through spot checks result in subsidy revocation and a 1-year ban from reapplication; currently 4,20,000 students benefit from this subsidy.

- Government scheme integration: EduForge partners with state and central government skill development schemes (PMKVY, AP Yuva Nestham, TS Youth Empowerment) where the government reimburses exam preparation costs; students enrolled in these schemes receive a unique scheme ID that they enter on EduForge to activate Premium access; the scheme body is billed quarterly based on the number of active students; this is a significant institutional sales channel contributing to 8% of EduForge's revenue.

- Institution scholarships (like TopRank's merit scholarship) are managed entirely by the institution — EduForge displays the scholarship criteria and the student's progress toward qualifying, but the award decision and fee waiver are processed in the institution's admin portal; EduForge's role is to surface the opportunity to the student and track eligibility criteria against their performance data.
