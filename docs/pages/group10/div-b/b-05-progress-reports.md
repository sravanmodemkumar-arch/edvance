# B-05 — Progress Reports & Rank Cards

> **URL:** `/student/performance/reports`
> **File:** `b-05-progress-reports.md`
> **Priority:** P2
> **Roles:** Student (S2–S6) · Parent (downloadable for minors) · Institution (generates school report cards)

---

## Overview

Downloadable, shareable, and printable academic reports consolidating performance across institutions and exam domains. Three report types: (1) **Institution Report Card** — school/coaching marks in the institution's format; (2) **Domain Performance Report** — monthly/quarterly summary for exam domains with AIR trend and topic mastery; (3) **Unified Progress Report** — cross-platform consolidated view unique to EduForge. Reports are auto-generated monthly and available on-demand. Parents of minors receive report PDFs via WhatsApp. Ravi's parents get a combined school + coaching report; Suresh generates his own SSC progress report to track his preparation.

## 5. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|--------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/reports` | List all auto-generated and custom reports |
| 2 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/reports/{id}` | View report detail (JSON for online, PDF link) |

## 6. Business Rules

- Unified Progress Reports are auto-generated on the 1st of every month at 2:00 AM IST for all 5 crore students — this is a batch job running on a dedicated report-generation cluster (32 worker nodes) that processes ~1,40,000 reports per minute; reports are pre-rendered as PDFs and stored in S3 with CloudFront distribution; the PDF includes a verification URL and QR code that resolves to a tamper-proof verification page showing the report metadata (student name, period, generation timestamp) — this prevents students from editing PDFs to inflate scores when sharing with parents, coaching centres, or scholarship committees.

- Institution report cards (school SA-1/SA-2, coaching monthly analysis) use the **institution's official template** — each institution uploads their report card template via the Institution Admin portal (Group 2/3/4/5) with merge fields for student data; EduForge fills in the data and generates the PDF in the institution's branded format; this means a Sri Chaitanya report card looks exactly like the official Sri Chaitanya format, not a generic EduForge template — critical for parent trust and institution brand consistency.

- Rank cards are generated in real-time immediately after test submission for Free students (score + AIR only) and within 2 minutes for Premium students (full section-wise analysis + rank card PDF); the rank card is shareable via WhatsApp and Instagram Story using pre-formatted image templates (optimised for 9:16 story format and 1:1 square post format) — social sharing of rank cards is the #1 organic growth driver for EduForge, accounting for 34% of new student sign-ups via referral links embedded in shared rank cards.

- Certificates are cryptographically signed using EduForge's RSA-2048 signing key — the verification URL `/verify/{code}` resolves to a public page that shows the certificate details and confirms "This certificate was issued by EduForge on [date] to [student name]"; the verification page does not require login and is accessible to anyone with the URL; this enables employers and institutions to verify certificates shared by students during admissions or job applications.

- For minors (under 18), monthly progress reports are automatically sent to the linked parent's WhatsApp number in addition to being available on the student's dashboard; the parent receives a PDF summary (not the full detailed report) with: overall score, rank, attendance, weak topics, and a "View Full Report" link that opens in the Parent Portal; the parent can opt out of monthly report WhatsApp messages from the Parent Portal settings, but the reports remain available online.
