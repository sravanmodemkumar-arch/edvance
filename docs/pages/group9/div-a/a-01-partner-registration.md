## 4. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|--------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | `POST` | `/api/v1/content-partner/register/` | Submit new partner registration |
| 2 | `GET` | `/api/v1/content-partner/register/partner-types/` | List partner types with metadata |
| 3 | `POST` | `/api/v1/content-partner/register/validate-pan/` | Validate PAN against NSDL database |
| 4 | `POST` | `/api/v1/content-partner/register/validate-gstin/` | Validate GSTIN against GST portal |
| 5 | `POST` | `/api/v1/content-partner/register/upload-document/` | Upload KYC document (PAN, Aadhaar, GST cert) |
| 6 | `GET` | `/api/v1/content-partner/register/{id}/status/` | Check registration status |
| 7 | `POST` | `/api/v1/content-partner/register/send-otp/` | Send mobile/email OTP for verification |
| 8 | `POST` | `/api/v1/content-partner/register/verify-otp/` | Verify OTP and confirm contact details |

## 5. Business Rules

- Partner registration follows a three-step funnel (type selection, details entry, document upload) that must be completable in under 10 minutes for an individual SME and under 15 minutes for a publisher or agency; abandonment at any step is tracked and triggers a follow-up email after 24 hours with a resume-registration link; Dr. Venkat Rao, a retired professor in Visakhapatnam, should not be forced to navigate a complex corporate registration flow — the Individual SME path skips GSTIN, CIN, and organisation fields entirely, presenting only personal credentials, academic qualifications, and PAN verification; conversely, Disha Publications must provide GSTIN and CIN because EduForge issues B2B invoices and must comply with GST input-tax-credit requirements.

- PAN verification is mandatory for all partner types because EduForge deducts TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) at 10% under Section 194J before remitting revenue share payments; the PAN entered during registration is validated in real-time against the NSDL/UTIITSL database to confirm the name matches (for individuals) or the entity name matches (for companies); a mismatched PAN blocks registration completion and displays a clear error ("PAN BNXPR1234A is registered to VENKAT RAO — please ensure your name matches exactly"); GSTIN validation for publishers and agencies cross-checks the GSTIN against the GST portal API to confirm active status, and extracts the legal name and registered address for auto-fill, reducing data entry errors.

- Duplicate detection runs at submission time using a composite key of PAN + mobile + email; if Dr. Venkat Rao attempts to register a second account with the same PAN BNXPR1234A, the system blocks the registration and presents a "You already have a partner account — please log in or contact support" message; for publishers, the duplicate check also includes GSTIN, so Disha Publications cannot create multiple partner accounts under the same GSTIN 07AABCD1234E1Z5; this prevents revenue-splitting schemes where a partner creates multiple accounts to circumvent quality thresholds or agreement terms.

- Document uploads are stored in an encrypted S3 bucket with server-side encryption (AES-256) and access restricted to the EduForge Content Team verification queue; uploaded documents are retained for 7 years per Income Tax Act record-keeping requirements; file types are restricted to PDF, JPG, and PNG with a maximum size of 5 MB per file; the system runs basic OCR validation on PAN card uploads to confirm the PAN number in the image matches the PAN entered in the form field; sample content uploads (mandatory for all partner types) are reviewed by the Content Team within 48 hours to assess question quality before the partner account is activated.
