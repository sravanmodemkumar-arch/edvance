## 5. Business Rules

- Custom reports containing student-level data (scores, attendance, personal details) are restricted by role; the Branch Manager can generate batch-level and branch-level reports; the Director can generate franchise-level and cross-branch reports; a Counsellor can generate only their own student case summaries; generating a report outside one's role scope is blocked by the system and logged as an access attempt; this tiered access ensures that sensitive student data (like who received emergency aid) cannot be exported by lower-access staff
- Scheduled reports are generated by the system at the configured time regardless of who is logged in; the system uses the role of the report creator (not the recipient) to determine what data scope is included; a scheduled report created by the Director will include franchise comparisons even when sent to the Branch Manager; the Branch Manager cannot create a scheduled report with Director-level data scope; schedule ownership is auditable — each scheduled report shows who created it and when
- All report exports are logged in an audit trail with the following fields: who generated it, when, what data sources were included, the date range, and whether it was downloaded or emailed; this audit trail is the DPDPA 2023 compliance record for data processing; TCC's data privacy policy commits to retaining export logs for 12 months; in the event of a data breach investigation, the export log allows TCC to identify which reports were generated and who received them
- Reports generated for regulatory or legal purposes (income tax filings, GST audits, consumer forum submissions) are flagged as "official" in the system; these reports have a tamper-evident footer that includes a hash of the report content and the generator's digital signature; if TCC submits a report to a consumer forum and the student claims the report was altered, the hash can be verified against the original; this integrity mechanism is particularly important for financial reports and grievance records
- The "Director's Board Report" template is the most sensitive report in the system; it includes full branch financials (net profit, staff costs, scholarship burden), faculty PIP status, all open grievances, and franchise performance comparisons; access to generate this report is limited to the Director and one designated administrator; it is never scheduled for email delivery (risk of interception) — the Director generates it manually and downloads it to a secure device; the report's presence in the system without an active download token expires after 30 minutes
