## 4. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `GET` | `/api/v1/parent/communicate/announcements/` | List announcements (filterable by child, institution, category) |
| 2 | `GET` | `/api/v1/parent/communicate/announcements/{id}/` | Get full announcement detail with attachments |
| 3 | `POST` | `/api/v1/parent/communicate/announcements/{id}/acknowledge/` | Mark announcement as read/acknowledged |
| 4 | `GET` | `/api/v1/parent/communicate/announcements/categories/` | List available announcement categories |
| 5 | `GET` | `/api/v1/parent/communicate/announcements/unread-count/` | Unread announcement count per institution |
| 6 | `PUT` | `/api/v1/parent/communicate/announcements/preferences/` | Update notification channel preferences |
| 7 | `GET` | `/api/v1/parent/communicate/announcements/search/` | Search announcements by keyword and date range |

## 5. Business Rules

- Announcements are scoped by the child's institution, department, section, and batch to ensure parents receive only relevant information; a parent with multiple children across institutions sees a unified feed but each announcement is tagged with the originating institution; when GCEH publishes a placement drive notice for B.Tech CSE 2026 batch, only parents of CSE students in the 2026 batch receive it, not parents of ECE students or 2027 batch; similarly, Sri Chaitanya section-level notices (like 11-A parent meeting) reach only parents of students in that section; this scoping prevents information overload and ensures parents do not receive dozens of irrelevant circulars daily.

- Fee reminder announcements are auto-generated by the fee management system and personalized per child, showing the exact amount due, breakdown, and payment status; these reminders are sent at three intervals: 15 days before due date, 7 days before due date, and on the due date itself; if payment is not received by the due date, a final reminder is sent 3 days after with the late fee amount added; fee reminders are always sent via all channels (in-app, email, and SMS) regardless of the parent's notification preference, because fee collection is critical for institution operations; the parent cannot unsubscribe from fee reminders, though they can acknowledge receipt to stop repeated SMS notifications.

- Exam schedule and hall ticket announcements follow a strict publication workflow; the Controller of Examinations or academic coordinator publishes the exam timetable, which must be approved by the principal or dean before it becomes visible to parents; once published, the announcement is immutable — it cannot be edited or deleted, only superseded by a new "revised" announcement that references the original; this immutability ensures that parents have a reliable record of exam dates, which is especially important in cases of disputes about exam scheduling or when students claim they were not informed; all exam-related announcements carry a mandatory read receipt, and the institution admin can see which parents have not yet acknowledged the notice.

- Urgent and emergency announcements bypass all notification preferences and are delivered immediately via all available channels including SMS and WhatsApp; this category covers campus closures due to natural disasters (cyclones are common in coastal Andhra Pradesh), bandh or hartal situations, security incidents, or sudden schedule changes like exam postponements; the emergency announcement can only be published by the principal, director, or designated emergency coordinator, preventing misuse of the urgent channel; when an emergency announcement is published, the system also triggers a push notification with a distinct alert tone, and the announcement appears as a full-screen overlay when the parent next opens the app, requiring explicit dismissal to ensure it is not missed.
