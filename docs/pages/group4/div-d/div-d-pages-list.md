## Regulatory Framework

- **UGC Hostel Guidelines 2016** — hostel committee composition, girls' hostel anti-ragging, CCTV requirements
- **AICTE Guidelines for Hostels** — separate hostels for boys/girls mandatory, warden residence on campus
- **FSSAI Licensing** — mess (canteen) requires FSSAI registration; inspection 2x/year; food safety officer
- **NBC 2016 (National Building Code)** — fire safety for residential blocks; evacuation drill 2x/year
- **POCSO Act 2012** — special considerations for girls' hostel security, access control, CCTV placement
- **UGC Anti-Ragging Regulations 2009** — hostel-specific anti-ragging: warden checks, helpline display, undertaking
- **Payment of Wages Act** — mess staff payment timeline compliance
- **EPF & ESI** — applicable to mess/housekeeping staff if >20 employees
