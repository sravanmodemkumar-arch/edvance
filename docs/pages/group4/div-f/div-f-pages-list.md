## Regulatory Framework

- **UGC (Promotion of Academic Research) Regulations 2025** — faculty API (Academic Performance Indicator) score for publications; UGC-CARE list as approved journals
- **NAAC Criterion 3** — Research, Innovations and Extension: publications, grants, patents, consultancy (highest-weight criterion for universities; significant for colleges)
- **NIRF Research Output** — Papers, citations, h-index (from Scopus/Web of Science); patent filing/granting; PhD awarded; project grants
- **AICTE Research Promotion Scheme** — funds for collaborative research (₹20L–₹2Cr range)
- **DST/SERB (Science & Engineering Research Board)** — major grant-givers; CRG (Core Research Grant), SIRE, Kiran schemes
- **DBT (Department of Biotechnology)** — applicable for biotech/bioinformatics branches
- **DRDO / ISRO / DAE** — defence/space research funding (requires security clearance for some projects)
- **CSR Funding** — Industry research contributions (Tax under Schedule VII CSR Act)
- **iThenticate / Turnitin** — plagiarism screening for publications; journals require <10–15% similarity
- **Copyright / Patent Act 1970** — IP ownership; provisional patent application; PCT for international
