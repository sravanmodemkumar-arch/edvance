## Regulatory Framework

- **UGC Pay Revision 2018** — Pay Matrix for college teachers (Academic Grade Pay — AGP); 7th CPC scales applicable for aided/UGC grant colleges; unaided colleges not legally bound but follow as best practice
- **UGC Minimum Qualifications Regulations 2018** — Assistant Professor: NET/SET + PG (60%); or PhD with NET exemption; Associate Professor: PhD + 8 years + API score; Professor: PhD + 10 years + API
- **AICTE Pay Scales 2019** — For AICTE-approved institutions; rationalized with UGC 7th CPC scales
- **DPDPA 2023** — Employee data (salary, performance) is sensitive; access controls for HR data
- **EPF Act 1952, ESI Act 1948** — Covered under G-06; HR module feeds payroll
- **Payment of Wages Act 1936** — Salary before 7th of following month (for wage <₹24,000); for higher salaries — before agreed date (usually last working day)
- **TDS Section 192** — Employer deducts TDS on salary; Form 16 by 15 June
- **Gratuity Act, Maternity Benefit Act** — HR events trigger these; covered in G-06
- **NAAC Criterion 6** — Governance, HR practices, appraisal systems as quality evidence
