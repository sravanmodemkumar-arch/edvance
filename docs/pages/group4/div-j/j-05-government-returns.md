## 6. Business Rules

- Government returns are not optional compliance activities — they are legal obligations; failure to file EPF ECR by the 15th triggers a daily penalty of ₹5/day/employee under the EPF Act; failure to file GST returns triggers late fees of ₹50/day (₹20/day for nil returns); a college with 100 employees that delays EPF for 30 days incurs ₹15,000 in penalties — avoidable with EduForge's automated reminders and pre-computed ECR data
- The AICTE EoA process is sequential and deadline-driven; missing the September application window means the college is without EoA for the following year until it is rectified — which creates legal issues for admissions, affiliation, and student scholarships; the EoA calendar must be treated as the institution's most important annual regulatory deadline
- JNTU affiliation is distinct from AICTE approval; both are required simultaneously; AICTE approval does not substitute for JNTU affiliation; a college with valid AICTE EoA but lapsed JNTU affiliation cannot legally admit students or conduct examinations under JNTU; the two processes have different timelines and different document requirements — EduForge tracks them independently
- Educational services are largely GST-exempt, but ancillary services (hostel, canteen, consultancy) have distinct GST treatment; many educational institutions incorrectly claim blanket GST exemption on all services including hostel and canteen, creating tax liability; GCEH's GST compliance correctly categorises each revenue stream; a GST audit can result in significant back-tax demands plus interest if misclassification is discovered
- The RTI Annual Report (filed by the SPIO to the State Information Commission) is a commonly overlooked return; under the Right to Information Act 2005, every public authority (including aided institutions — GCEH is unaided but voluntarily transparent) must publish an annual proactive disclosure report; GCEH files this voluntarily as a good governance practice; the NAAC Criterion 6 (Governance) awards marks for transparency and RTI-compliant information disclosure
