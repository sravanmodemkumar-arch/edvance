## Regulatory Framework

- **AICTE Act 1987 + AICTE Approval Process Handbook** — annual EoA (Extension of Approval), intake approval, mandatory disclosure
- **UGC Act 1956** — UGC recognition for degree-granting; Section 2(f) / 12B status
- **NAAC (National Assessment & Accreditation Council)** — 7-criterion NAAC assessment; AQAR annual submission; 5-year cycle
- **NBA (National Board of Accreditation)** — programme-level accreditation; Washington Accord compliance for B.Tech; outcome-based education (OBE)
- **UGC Anti-Ragging Regulations 2009** — mandatory cell, helpline (1800-180-5522), undertakings, CCTV, action protocols
- **POSH Act 2013 (Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace)** — ICC (Internal Complaints Committee) mandatory; annual report to District Officer; employer liability
- **EPF Act 1952, ESI Act 1948, Payment of Wages Act 1936** — statutory registers for labour compliance
- **NBC 2016 (National Building Code)** — structural safety, fire NOC, lift safety, accessibility
- **Telangana Fire Services Act** — fire NOC renewal; inspection protocol
- **CERT-In (Cyber Security)** — IT systems of educational institutions; data breach notification 72 hours
- **NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework)** — annual ranking submission; 5-parameter framework
