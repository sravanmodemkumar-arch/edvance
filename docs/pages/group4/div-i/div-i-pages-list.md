## Regulatory Framework

- **UGC Guidelines on Student Welfare** — mandatory grievance redressal mechanism; 30-day SLA
- **UGC Anti-Ragging Regulations 2009** — student-facing reporting; mentoring; prevention
- **POSH Act 2013** — student-facing ICC complaint process; awareness
- **RPwD Act 2016** — equal opportunity for PwD students; reasonable accommodation; grievance
- **SC/ST Prevention of Atrocities Act 1989 + UGC Equal Opportunity** — SC/ST Cell mandatory in all colleges; remedial coaching; reservation benefit monitoring
- **Mental Health Care Act 2017** — right to mental health care; no stigma; counsellor confidentiality
- **NSS (National Service Scheme) Rules** — 2 units at GCEH; 240 hours/year per enrolled student
- **NCC Act 1948** — NCC contingent; Annual Training Camp; certificate value for government jobs
- **DPDPA 2023** — counselling records are health/sensitive data; special category protection; no disclosure without consent
