## Regulatory Framework

- **NAAC Criterion 5.2** — Student Progression: Placement rates, higher education, government jobs, entrepreneurship (key accreditation metric)
- **NIRF Ranking** — Graduation Outcomes (GO) parameter: Placement + salary data reported annually
- **UGC Guidelines for Placement Cells** — Placement cell composition, industry interface
- **AICTE EAC (Employability Assessment Cell)** — Annual employability survey
- **Labour Laws (for internship)** — Internship ≠ employment; AICTE Internship Policy 2021 (mandatory 4-week internship for B.Tech final year); stipend if any attracts TDS if >₹50,000
- **DPDPA 2023** — Resume/profile data shared with recruiters requires student consent; bulk data export to company recruitment portals must be consent-gated
- **Persons with Disabilities Act 2016** — Placement cell must actively facilitate placement of PwD students; cannot discriminate in campus recruitment process
- **SC/ST Students** — Placement cell should facilitate access to PSU/government sector placements where reservation benefits apply
