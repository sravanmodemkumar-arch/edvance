## Division Purpose

Manages the complete student journey in a college — from application and admission through to graduation and alumni records. College-specific contexts:
- Multiple degree programmes (UG 3-year, UG 4-year NEP, PG, Diploma, PhD)
- University affiliation requirements (student registration with affiliating university, hall tickets, convocation)
- Entrance-based admissions (NEET, JEE, CAT, state-level counselling)
- Category reservations (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD — as per Government of India norms)
- Merit and management quota management
- Academic Bank of Credits (ABC) under NEP 2020
