## 1. Purpose

Affiliated colleges must register their students with the affiliating university for every semester's exams and receive hall tickets. This module manages:
- University exam fee collection from students
- Data submission to university portal (batch upload)
- Hall ticket generation and distribution
- Backlog (supplementary) exam registration
- Detained student handling (not registered for detained subjects)
- Results notification (university releases results; EduForge fetches and displays)

## 6. Business Rules

- Hall ticket is mandatory to enter the exam hall; a student without a hall ticket cannot appear; if a student's hall ticket has a data error (wrong name, wrong photo), the Examination Controller must request a correction from JNTU before the exam — this cannot wait until exam day
- Detained students must NOT be registered for detained subjects; registering a detained student for university exams and allowing them to appear is a serious violation that can result in those results being cancelled and the college's affiliation being questioned
- Backlog exams are conducted by the university, not the college; the college's role is registration (fee collection + data upload) and providing the venue (if the university designates the college as an exam centre); the college cannot conduct its own backlog exam in place of the university exam
- Examination fees collected from students must be remitted to the university within the prescribed deadline (JNTU: within 7 days of collection period close); delay in remittance attracts penalties and may delay hall ticket generation for those students
