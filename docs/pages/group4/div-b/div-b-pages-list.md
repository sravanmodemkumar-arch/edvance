## Division Purpose

Academic management covers everything related to curriculum, teaching, assessment, and quality at the department level. College-specific:
- Multiple departments with HODs
- Lesson plans and course files (NAAC evidence)
- Internal marks (CIE — Continuous Internal Evaluation) separate from university exams
- Question paper management (internal exams — security)
- Faculty feedback (student-to-faculty — mandatory for NAAC)
- Academic calendar (semester-based)
- Research-linked academic activities (Ph.D. supervision, publications)
