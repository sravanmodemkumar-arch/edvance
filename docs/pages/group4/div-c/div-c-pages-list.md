## Division Purpose

College fee management differs significantly from school fee management:
- AICTE/AFRC fee regulation (fee cap enforced by government committee)
- Multiple fee components: tuition, hostel, exam, development fee, lab fee, library fee
- Scholarship and fee waiver accounting (NSP, state ePASS — tuition fee reimbursement)
- GST applicability (most educational services exempt; ancillary services may attract GST)
- University examination fee collection and remittance
- Bank/NBFC student loan disbursements (fee paid directly by bank)
- Demand draft/RTGS processing for management quota payments
