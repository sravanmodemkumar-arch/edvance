## 1. Purpose

Consolidated welfare reporting across all J-division sub-modules. These reports serve:
- **CBSE affiliation inspection:** Welfare programmes, counsellor availability, CWSN support, anti-ragging compliance
- **Annual school report / prospectus:** Achievements, welfare initiatives (positive publicity)
- **Management review:** Principal uses trend data to allocate welfare resources, identify systemic issues
- **NCPCR compliance:** Child rights and welfare documentation
- **Parent Trust:** Summary welfare report shared at annual PTM (aggregate — no individual data)

## 8. Business Rules

- The annual welfare report (CBSE format) is signed by the Principal and must be available for inspection; the counsellor's data feeds into it but the report is the Principal's document
- Counselling analytics are fully anonymised — even the Principal's view shows class-level and category trends without individual names; the counsellor uses this for their own professional review
- POCSO case counts appear in the annual welfare report as a number only (e.g., "3 cases reported") — no case details, student names, or outcomes appear in any report outside J-02
- Parent-facing summary (PTM handout) is reviewed by the Principal before distribution; it must not inadvertently identify any student or family (e.g., "1 student had a POCSO case" is not acceptable even without a name if it enables identification in a small class)
- Welfare analytics are retained for 5 years; they inform year-on-year comparison and school improvement planning
- Any reports generated for NCPCR, CBSE inspection, or government submission are tracked in the log; the school must be able to evidence what was submitted and when
