## Key Regulatory Context

- **POCSO Act 2012 (Protection of Children from Sexual Offences):** Mandatory reporting to DCPU within 24 hours; school is mandated reporter; counsellor confidentiality does NOT override POCSO mandatory reporting; Sections 19–21 (duty to report, penalty for non-reporting)
- **CBSE Circular on Counselling:** Every school must have a trained counsellor; counsellor must not double as teacher (in schools >500 students); confidentiality of counselling records is mandated
- **UGC/NCERT Anti-Ragging guidelines:** Anti-Ragging Committee mandatory; affidavit from every student (Class IX+) and parents at the start of each year
- **RTE Act Section 17:** Corporal punishment is prohibited; any teacher inflicting corporal punishment must be disciplined; student rights to dignified treatment
- **NCPCR guidelines:** Child rights; student grievance redressal within 30 days
- **NEP 2020:** Holistic development; counsellor in every school; career guidance from Class IX
- **DPDPA 2023:** Counselling session notes are among the most sensitive personal data — access is restricted to the counsellor and the Principal only; class teacher sees only the welfare flag (referred/in-counselling), not the session content
