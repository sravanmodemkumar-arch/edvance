## 1. Purpose

The daily pre-trip safety checklist ensures every bus is roadworthy and safe before students board. Required by:
- **Supreme Court 2012 school bus guidelines:** Pre-trip inspection mandatory
- **CBSE Circular (Transport Safety):** Checklist must be maintained and signed daily
- **Motor Vehicles Act 1988:** Driver is personally responsible for vehicle roadworthiness before a trip
- **Insurance:** If an accident occurs and there is no pre-trip inspection record, insurance can repudiate the claim (contributory negligence)

Two checklists:
1. **Driver checklist (technical):** Vehicle mechanical and safety equipment
2. **Escort checklist (student safety):** Student boarding, seating, emergency awareness

## 8. Business Rules

- A driver who has not submitted the morning checklist cannot mark the route as started in the GPS system — the checklist is a hard prerequisite; exception can be overridden by Transport In-Charge with a reason logged (emergency situations only)
- If the morning checklist flags a critical item (brake issue, tyre failure, fire extinguisher empty, GPS not working), the bus is grounded until the item is resolved; the Transport In-Charge must arrange a substitute vehicle; parents are notified
- Missing checklist >3 times in a month: written warning to driver, logged in I-04 profile
- Post-route check (no student left on bus) is mandatory and non-negotiable; a child left on a hot bus is potentially fatal; the driver must physically walk the full bus length after every route and confirm in the app
- CCTV not recording: if the escort's checklist flags CCTV as non-functional, the route can still operate but the repair must be completed within 24 hours; operating >48 hours without CCTV is a compliance violation (Supreme Court 2012)
- Checklist data is retained for 3 years (matches I-05 bus attendance retention period); incident-linked checklists are retained indefinitely
- All checklist submissions are timestamped and GPS-stamped (driver's phone location must be near the bus depot/school at submission time — soft validation with alert if mismatched)
