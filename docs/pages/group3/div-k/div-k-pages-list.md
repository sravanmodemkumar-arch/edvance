## Key Regulatory Framework

- **CBSE Affiliation Bye-Laws 2018:** Detailed requirements for school infrastructure, staff qualifications, fee structure, student records, and examination conduct
- **RTE Act 2009:** 25% EWS/DG seats; no-detention policy (Classes I–VIII); teacher qualifications (TET); pupil-teacher ratio; infrastructure standards (classrooms, toilets, drinking water, playground)
- **Fire NOC:** State fire department NOC required for school buildings; annual renewal; fire drills minimum 2/year
- **Building Safety:** State government school safety guidelines; structural audit requirements; NDMA (National Disaster Management Authority) school safety programme
- **DPDPA 2023:** Data Protection obligations — school is a "Data Fiduciary"; must implement consent management, data minimisation, breach reporting within 72 hours (proposed rules), and appoint a Data Protection Officer if processing significant volumes
- **POCSO Act 2012:** Already covered in J-02; compliance registers here
- **Motor Vehicles Act:** School transport compliance (covered in Division I; compliance dashboard here)
- **FSSAI:** Canteen/mess food safety licence (covered in H-06; licence tracking here)
- **State education department regulations:** Vary by state; typically require recognitions, service books, school register formats prescribed by state
