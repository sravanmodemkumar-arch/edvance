## 1. Purpose

Manages all aspects of CBSE school affiliation — from initial affiliation application, upgrades (up-gradation from Class X to XII), annual renewal, and inspection readiness. CBSE affiliation is the school's licence to operate; losing affiliation means students cannot write CBSE Board exams.

CBSE Affiliation Bye-Laws 2018 cover:
- Infrastructure standards (minimum area, classrooms, labs, library, toilets, sports)
- Staff qualifications (teacher eligibility, TET requirement, Principal qualification)
- Fee structure submission to CBSE
- Student-teacher ratio
- Examination fee collection norms
- Probationary vs regular affiliation status

Affiliation types:
- **Provisional affiliation:** First 3 years; school must achieve benchmarks
- **Regular affiliation:** After successful provisional period; 5-year cycle
- **Upgradation:** When school adds Class XI–XII to an existing Class X school
- **Continuation:** Annual renewal within the 5-year regular cycle

## 8. Business Rules

- Any condition flagged by CBSE (even Grade A schools receive conditions) must be tracked with a resolution plan and deadline; EduForge auto-reminds the Affiliation Coordinator 30 days before each condition deadline
- Fee structure submitted to CBSE must match exactly what is charged to students (D-04 fee structure); any discrepancy between CBSE-filed fees and actual charges is a serious affiliation violation
- CBSE OASIS portal (external system) is the authority; EduForge mirrors the status locally; the Affiliation Coordinator must manually update EduForge after CBSE portal submissions
- Affiliation lapse: if affiliation is not renewed and lapses, existing students are protected for one year (CBSE provides time to transition); but no new students may be admitted; this scenario generates a P0 alert in EduForge
- Annual fee submission: schools that miss the October deadline receive a penalty notice from CBSE; multiple missed submissions are a ground for affiliation show-cause; the EduForge calendar (A-10) creates a reminder on 1 September each year
- Inspection grade below B: school is placed on probationary affiliation; probationary schools must submit quarterly compliance reports to CBSE; EduForge tracks this status and generates the quarterly reports
