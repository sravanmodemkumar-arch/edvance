## Key Regulatory Context

- **Contract Labour Act 1970:** Applicable if school uses contract workers (canteen, housekeeping, security)
- **Payment of Wages Act 1936 + Minimum Wages Act 1948:** Salary disbursement by 7th of following month; minimum wage compliance for support staff
- **Gratuity Act 1972:** Gratuity payable after 5 years of continuous service; (15 days × basic salary × years of service)/26
- **Provident Fund (EPF) Act 1952:** PF deduction for all employees earning ≤₹15,000 basic (mandatory); voluntary for higher earners; 12% employee + 12% employer (8.33% EPS + 3.67% EPF)
- **Professional Tax (PT):** State tax on salaries; TS Professional Tax slab applies
- **TDS Section 192:** Tax deducted at source on salary; Form 16 issued by April 30 every year
- **Leave rules:** No mandatory national leave law for private schools but CBSE and state education department norms apply; Common: Earned Leave (EL), Casual Leave (CL), Sick Leave (SL), Maternity Leave (26 weeks under Maternity Benefit Amendment 2017), Paternity Leave (school policy)
- **Service book:** Government-style service book for each teacher; mandatory for state-recognised schools; records promotions, increments, leave, disciplinary actions
