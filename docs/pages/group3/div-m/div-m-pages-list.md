## Division Purpose

The MIS (Management Information System) division aggregates data from all other divisions (A through L, plus student and parent data) into consolidated dashboards and reports for school leadership and the school trust/management. This is the "command centre" of EduForge — every module feeds into MIS.

Key audiences:
- **Principal:** Daily operational pulse + strategic trends
- **VP:** Academic performance trends across classes and subjects
- **Trust/Management:** Multi-school comparison, financial health, compliance health
- **CBSE / Regulatory:** Pre-built inspection-ready report packages
- **School improvement planning:** Data-driven decision support

## Integration Map

MIS pulls from every division:
- **A–C:** Student data, admissions, section performance
- **D:** Fee collection, outstanding, financial health
- **E:** Student attendance (daily, monthly, annual)
- **F–H:** Parent communication, events, academic calendar
- **I:** Transport compliance and incidents
- **J:** Welfare flags, counselling load, grievances
- **K:** Compliance scores, inspection readiness
- **L:** Staff attendance, payroll cost, training, appraisal
