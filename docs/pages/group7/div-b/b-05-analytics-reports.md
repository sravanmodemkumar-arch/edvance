## 4. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/admin/analytics/performance/?batch=APPSC-Mar26&period=30d` | Student performance analytics |
| 2 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/admin/analytics/test/{id}/` | Per-test analytics with question insights |
| 3 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/admin/analytics/revenue/?month=2026-03` | Revenue report for a given month |
| 4 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/admin/analytics/batch-comparison/` | Batch-wise comparison metrics |
| 5 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/admin/analytics/export/?type=revenue&format=csv` | Export analytics data (CSV/PDF) |
| 6 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/admin/analytics/question-insights/{test_id}/` | Question-level difficulty and flag data |

## 5. Business Rules

- Revenue reports show gross collection (what students paid) and net revenue (what the TSP receives after EduForge's platform fee and payment gateway charges); EduForge's platform fee varies by plan tier: 15% for Enterprise TSPs, 18% for Standard (TopRank's tier), and 22% for Basic; the fee is calculated on each transaction, not on monthly aggregate, because different students may be on different subscription plans; settlement happens on the 10th of the following month — TopRank Academy receives Rs.3,42,880 for March on 10th April via bank transfer; the TSP Admin can view settlement history and download monthly invoices for their accounting
- Question-level analytics serve dual purposes: identifying weak topics for students and identifying bad questions for the content team; if Q47 (AP Geography — Rivers) has only 18.2% accuracy and the most-chosen wrong answer (Option B at 42.3%) is close to the correct answer, it might indicate an ambiguous question rather than a hard topic; when 14 students flag Q23, the system creates a review task for the TSP's content reviewer; a question with below 15% accuracy and multiple flags is auto-quarantined (hidden from future mocks) until the reviewer approves or removes it; this feedback loop continuously improves the TSP's question quality
- Batch-wise comparison is the primary tool TSPs use to evaluate their coaching effectiveness; if BANK-Jan26 batch shows a "Declining" trend while APPSC-Mar26 shows "Improving," the TSP Admin investigates: is the Banking faculty underperforming, are the Banking questions too hard, or have the Banking students disengaged; the trend is calculated as a 4-week rolling average of batch mean scores — "Improving" means the rolling average has increased for 3 consecutive weeks, "Declining" means it has decreased for 3 consecutive weeks, "Stable" means variance is within 2%; TSPs in Vijayawada compete on results, so this data directly influences faculty retention and batch strategy
- Export functionality supports CSV and PDF formats; CSV exports are used by TSP accountants for reconciliation with their Tally/Zoho Books accounting software — the CSV includes transaction ID, student name, plan, amount, gateway reference, and GST breakup; PDF exports are formatted reports with the TSP's branding (TopRank Academy logo, header) suitable for sharing with coaching centre management or investors; all exports include a "Generated on" timestamp and "Data as of" timestamp to prevent confusion when reports are shared weeks later; exports are rate-limited to 10 per hour per TSP to prevent automated scraping of student data
