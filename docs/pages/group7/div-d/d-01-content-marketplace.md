## 4. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/marketplace/` | Browse EduForge content pool with filters |
| 2 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/marketplace/exams/` | List all exams with question counts and licence status |
| 3 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/marketplace/preview/{exam_id}/` | Preview sample questions for an exam category |
| 4 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/marketplace/preview/{question_id}/` | Preview single question with watermark |
| 5 | `POST` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/marketplace/request-addon/` | Request adding an exam/subject to current licence |
| 6 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/exchange/` | Browse TSP-to-TSP marketplace listings |
| 7 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/exchange/{listing_id}/` | View listing details and seller profile |
| 8 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/exchange/{listing_id}/preview/` | Preview sample from TSP listing (25 Qs max) |
| 9 | `POST` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/exchange/cart/` | Add listing to cart |
| 10 | `POST` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/exchange/purchase/` | Complete TSP marketplace purchase |
| 11 | `POST` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/exchange/listings/` | Create a new TSP content listing (sell) |
| 12 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/exchange/my-listings/` | View own TSP listings and sales stats |
| 13 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/exchange/my-purchases/` | View purchased TSP content and licence status |
| 14 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/content/marketplace/stats/` | Dashboard stats — licensed vs total, usage breakdown |

## 5. Business Rules

- The EduForge content marketplace operates on a tiered licensing model specifically designed for the Indian competitive exam preparation ecosystem; the Basic tier at Rs 5 per student per month provides access to 2,00,000 questions covering major state PSC exams (APPSC, TSPSC) and SSC CGL, which is sufficient for a small regional coaching centre; the Standard tier at Rs 12 per student per month unlocks 8,00,000 questions adding IBPS, RRB, and partial coverage of UPSC, which suits mid-sized centres like TopRank Academy with 3,000 students targeting multiple exam categories; the Premium tier at Rs 20 per student per month grants access to the full 18,42,000+ pool including niche exams like KPSC KAS, Defence (NDA/CDS/AFCAT), and all state PSCs; tier pricing is per-active-student per month (students who logged in at least once in the billing cycle), not per-registered-student, which is fairer for TSPs with seasonal fluctuations typical in Indian coaching where enrolments spike before exam notification dates

- Question preview and sampling is strictly controlled to prevent content theft; TSP admins and faculty can preview up to 5 questions at a time from any exam category they have not yet licensed, and each preview is watermarked with the TSP name and timestamp; bulk download of preview questions is not permitted — only a 50-question sample set (watermarked) can be downloaded for internal evaluation; this is necessary because EduForge's content pool is its primary competitive advantage and content piracy is endemic in the Indian edtech space — competing platforms have been known to hire people to screenshot and manually copy question banks; preview watermarking, screenshot detection (on the web app), and audit logging of every preview action provide a multi-layered defence against content extraction

- The TSP-to-TSP content exchange marketplace enables coaching centres with strong original content to monetise their question banks while remaining on the EduForge platform; a TSP like KSR Academy (Guntur, AP) which has invested years building 12,000 original APPSC questions can list them for other TSPs to licence at a per-student-per-month rate; EduForge takes a 20% platform commission on marketplace transactions and handles billing, content delivery, and dispute resolution; all marketplace content must pass EduForge Content Team verification (quality score of 4.0 or above, no copyright violations, no duplicate questions from the EduForge pool) before listing goes live; this creates a network effect — the more TSPs join EduForge, the richer the marketplace becomes, making it harder for TSPs to leave

- Content in the marketplace is region-aware and language-tagged to serve India's diverse exam landscape; each question is tagged with up to 4 languages (English, Hindi, and regional languages like Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati), and TSPs can filter by language availability; this is critical because state PSC exams (APPSC, TSPSC, KPSC, TNPSC, WBPSC) require questions in regional languages, while central exams (SSC, IBPS, RRB) are typically in English and Hindi; EduForge's content team ensures that translations are done by subject-matter experts (not machine-translated) and each translated question goes through a separate quality review; TSPs in Andhra Pradesh like TopRank Academy will see Telugu-available content prioritised in their marketplace results, while a TSP in Karnataka will see Kannada-first sorting
