## 3. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/billing/plans/` | List all available EduForge subscription plans |
| 2 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/billing/plans/current/` | Get TSP's current active plan and usage |
| 3 | `POST` | `/api/v1/tsp/billing/plans/upgrade/` | Initiate plan upgrade with prorated billing |
| 4 | `POST` | `/api/v1/tsp/billing/plans/downgrade/` | Schedule plan downgrade at cycle end |
| 5 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/billing/plans/preview/{target_plan}/` | Preview prorated charges for plan change |
| 6 | `GET` | `/api/v1/tsp/billing/content-licence/` | Get content licence status and usage |
| 7 | `PATCH` | `/api/v1/tsp/billing/content-licence/` | Update content licence student count |
| 8 | `POST` | `/api/v1/tsp/billing/plans/annual/` | Switch from monthly to annual billing |

## 5. Business Rules

- EduForge subscription plans are tiered by student capacity, not by feature gating alone; the Starter plan at ₹9,999/month supports up to 1,000 active students, Growth at ₹24,999/month supports up to 5,000 active students, and Enterprise at ₹49,999/month supports unlimited students; "active student" is defined as any student who has logged in at least once in the current billing cycle or has an active paid subscription with the TSP; dormant students (no login for 90+ days and no active subscription) do not count toward the cap; this prevents TSPs from being penalised for students who signed up once and never returned; the student count is checked daily at 00:00 IST and if it exceeds the plan limit for 3 consecutive days, the TSP Admin receives an upgrade notice with a 7-day grace period before access restrictions apply

- Plan upgrades are immediate and prorated to the day; when a TSP upgrades mid-cycle, the unused portion of the current plan is credited and the new plan is charged for the remaining days of the cycle; this proration uses calendar days (not 30-day months) so a mid-March upgrade in a 31-day month calculates differently than mid-February; the prorated amount is collected immediately via the TSP's registered payment method (Razorpay auto-debit mandate); plan downgrades, however, are never prorated — the TSP continues to enjoy the higher plan until the current billing cycle ends, and the lower plan takes effect from the next cycle; this asymmetry (instant upgrade, deferred downgrade) is standard SaaS practice and prevents gaming where a TSP upgrades for one day to access Enterprise features and immediately downgrades

- The content licence fee is separate from the platform subscription and is calculated as ₹5 per active student per month; this fee grants the TSP access to EduForge's entire question pool of 18,00,000+ questions, previous year papers, daily practice sets, and current affairs content; the content licence count is synced with actual student enrolments — if TopRank has 3,000 students in March but enrols 500 more in April, the April content licence automatically adjusts to 3,500 students (₹17,500/month); the content licence has a minimum of 500 students regardless of actual count, so a new TSP with 200 students still pays ₹2,500/month for content; content licence renewal is annual but billed monthly, and the TSP must give 30 days' notice to cancel the content licence

- Annual billing offers a 2-month discount (pay for 10 months, get 12) on platform subscriptions; the annual amount is collected upfront via bank transfer (NEFT/RTGS to EduForge's ICICI Bank current account) or via Razorpay subscription with a single annual charge; once on annual billing, the TSP cannot switch back to monthly until the annual term ends; early termination of an annual plan incurs a penalty equal to the discount received (e.g., if Growth annual saves ₹49,998 over monthly, early termination forfeits that discount and the TSP is charged as if they were on monthly billing for the months used); annual plans auto-renew 30 days before expiry with an email + SMS reminder at 60, 30, and 7 days before renewal; the TSP Admin can cancel auto-renewal from this page at any time before the 30-day mark
