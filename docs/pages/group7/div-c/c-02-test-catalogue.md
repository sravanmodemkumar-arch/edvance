## 4. API Endpoints

| # | Method | Endpoint | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/tests/` | List all available tests with filters (exam, type, status) |
| 2 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/tests/{test_id}/` | Test detail (pattern, subjects, schedule, stats) |
| 3 | `POST` | `/api/v1/student/tests/{test_id}/enroll/` | Enroll in a specific test |
| 4 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/tests/categories/` | List exam categories with test counts |
| 5 | `GET` | `/api/v1/student/subscriptions/plans/` | Available subscription plans and pricing for the TSP |
| 6 | `POST` | `/api/v1/student/subscriptions/checkout/` | Initiate Razorpay checkout for a plan or test series |
| 7 | `POST` | `/api/v1/student/subscriptions/verify-payment/` | Verify Razorpay payment callback and activate plan |
| 8 | `POST` | `/api/v1/student/coupons/validate/` | Validate a coupon code and return discount amount |

## 5. Business Rules

- Test visibility is controlled by the TSP admin through a three-tier access model: FREE tests are visible and attemptable by all registered students regardless of subscription status; PREMIUM tests are visible to all students (to create aspiration) but locked behind a paywall showing the pricing modal; HIDDEN tests are invisible in the catalogue and only accessible via a direct link shared by the TSP admin, which is used for special batches or scholarship tests; the TSP admin configures each test's access tier during creation (B-04) and can change it at any time — changing a PREMIUM test to FREE mid-window does not reset attempts by students who already paid, but it does allow unpaid students to attempt it for the remainder of the window; a test's tier is stored as an enum (free, premium, hidden) in the `test_access_tier` column and evaluated at query time against the student's active subscription
- Pricing displayed in the catalogue is entirely controlled by the TSP; TopRank Academy sets its own prices (Rs.299/month, Rs.799/quarter, Rs.2,499/annual) through the TSP admin panel (B-04); EduForge provides a recommended pricing engine that suggests prices based on the TSP's city tier, student count, and competitor analysis, but the TSP is free to ignore it; when a student pays Rs.299, the full amount is collected by EduForge's Razorpay account and settled to the TSP's bank account after deducting the platform commission (15% for Standard plan, 20% for Starter, 25% for Enterprise with premium support); GST at 18% is applied on the platform commission, not on the full amount, because EduForge is the technology service provider and the TSP is the education service provider; the student's invoice shows the TSP's GSTIN and brand, not EduForge's
- Enrollment in a scheduled test (like Mock #13 with a fixed window) creates a slot reservation that is counted against the TSP's concurrent test capacity; TopRank Academy on the Standard plan can have up to 500 concurrent test-takers; if 500 students are already enrolled in a test window that overlaps, the 501st student sees a "Test Full — please contact TopRank Academy support" message; the TSP admin is alerted when enrollment reaches 80% of capacity (400 students) so they can either upgrade their plan, extend the test window, or create a second batch; for open tests with no fixed window (like the subject test with a week-long deadline), there is no concurrency cap because students take it at different times; the capacity constraint only applies to tests with a window shorter than 4 hours
- Coupon codes are created and managed by the TSP admin (Division B) and are scoped to the TSP's tenant; a coupon like "TOPRANK50" offering 50% off the quarterly plan can only be redeemed by students on TopRank Academy's portal — it will not work on any other TSP's portal even if guessed; coupons support four types: percentage discount (up to 100%), flat discount (e.g., Rs.200 off), free trial extension (e.g., 7 extra days), and full unlock (100% discount for scholarship students); each coupon has a usage limit (global and per-student), an expiry date, and optional restrictions by exam category or plan type; the validation endpoint returns the discounted amount in real-time so the checkout page shows the final price before the student initiates payment; expired or exhausted coupons return a user-friendly error ("This coupon has expired" or "This coupon has reached its usage limit")
